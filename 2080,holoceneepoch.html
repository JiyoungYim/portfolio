2080, Third Molar from the Holocene Epoch

2023, mixed media installation

The third molars, also known as Wisdom teeth are expected to be disappeared in fifty years according to some scientists. This work is a speculation on the Museum of The Third molar tooth, situated in the year 2080, based on the premise of the disappearance of human wisdom teeth. This work started from the question of how the third molars is going to be remembered when it disappears.
